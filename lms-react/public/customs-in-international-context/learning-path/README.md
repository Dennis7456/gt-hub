# Customs in International Context - International Trade Learning Path

## Overview
Duolingo-style gamified learning path covering comprehensive content on international trade, customs procedures, trade documentation, payment methods, transport modes, Incoterms, and EPA agreements.

## Course Structure

### Total Content
- **12 Units**: Progressive learning from foundations to mastery
- **60 Lessons**: Mix of learning, practice, and assessment
- **140 Flashcards**: Real educational content from flashcard database
- **Estimated Time**: 36 days (recommended pace)
- **Total XP Available**: 895+ base XP

### Learning Path Progression

#### Unit 1: What International Trade Is (2 days)
- Meaning of international trade
- Import, export, and trade balance
- Core trade terms (HS Code, tariff, dumping, etc.)
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 2: Why Countries Trade (3 days)
- Causes of international trade
- Advantages and disadvantages
- Economic logic and risk analysis
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 3: Types of Trade and Trade Structure (2 days)
- Import, export, and re-export trade
- Bilateral and multilateral trade
- Trade classification
- 4 lessons | 60 XP

#### Unit 4: Players in International Trade (3 days)
- Commercial players (sellers, buyers, agents)
- Transport and customs players
- Banks and consolidators
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 5: Steps in International Trade (3 days)
- Order and confirmation stages
- Shipment and payment processes
- Customs clearance procedures
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 6: Documents Used in International Trade (5 days)
- Five document categories
- Commercial documents (invoices, contracts, packing lists)
- Transport documents (bills of lading, airway bills, manifests)
- Document application scenarios
- 6 lessons | 95 XP

#### Unit 7: Methods of Payment in International Trade (4 days)
- Cash in advance and letters of credit
- Documentary collections (D/P and D/A)
- Open account and consignment
- Risk allocation analysis
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 8: Modes of Transport and Logistics Choice (3 days)
- Main transport modes (air, maritime, rail, road, pipeline)
- Transport selection criteria
- Intermodal integration
- 5 lessons | 85 XP

#### Unit 9: Incoterms 2020 (5 days)
- Purpose and scope of Incoterms
- What Incoterms cover and don't cover
- Key changes in Incoterms 2020
- Classification of 11 rules
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 10: The 11 Incoterms in Practice (5 days)
- Any-mode rules (EXW, FCA, CPT, CIP, DAP, DPU, DDP)
- Sea/inland waterway rules (FAS, FOB, CFR, CIF)
- Practical application scenarios
- 5 lessons | 85 XP

#### Unit 11: Kenya-EU and Kenya-UK EPAs (4 days)
- Kenya-EU EPA objectives and implementation
- Kenya-UK EPA structure and tariff treatment
- Challenges and mitigation strategies
- 5 lessons | 75 XP

#### Unit 12: Grand Mastery Path (4 days)
- Integrated process review
- Risk and responsibility synthesis
- Comprehensive trade scenarios
- Final mastery assessment
- 5 lessons | 95 XP

## Lesson Types

### Learn (10 XP)
Interactive content presentation with flashcard-based material covering key concepts and definitions.

### Match (10 XP)
Pair matching exercises connecting terms, concepts, and examples.

### Quiz (15 XP)
Multiple-choice questions testing comprehension and recall.

### Scenario (20 XP)
Real-world application exercises requiring practical decision-making.

### Checkpoint (30 XP)
Comprehensive unit assessments demonstrating mastery.

## Gamification Features

### XP System
- Earn XP for completing lessons
- Perfect lesson bonus: +5 XP
- Streak bonus (every 5 days): +20 XP
- Track progress toward mastery

### Hearts System
- Start with 5 hearts
- Lose 1 heart per wrong answer
- Recover hearts through:
  - Practice old lessons
  - Daily review
  - Wait timer

### Badges
- Trade Terms Rookie (Units 1-2)
- Transaction Tracker (Units 3-5)
- Documentation Specialist (Unit 6)
- Trade Finance Analyst (Unit 7)
- Logistics Navigator (Unit 8)
- Incoterms Strategist (Units 9-10)
- EPA Interpreter (Unit 11)
- International Trade Master (Unit 12)

### Streak Rewards
- 3 days: Bonus review chest
- 7 days: Checkpoint retry without heart loss
- 14 days: Legendary review unlock
- 30 days: Course mastery badge

## Review System

### Daily Practice
- Recycles weak lessons
- Reviews commonly confused concepts
- Target: 30 XP per day

### Spaced Repetition
- Same day review after first completion
- Next day review if score < 80%
- 3-day review for new concepts
- 7-day review for checkpoint skills
- 14-day mastery refresh

## Course Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:
1. Define international trade and key related terms
2. Identify players involved in international trade transactions
3. Outline the main steps in international trade transactions and the documents used
4. Explain methods of payment, modes of transport, Incoterms, and selected trade agreements
