# Customs Clearance I Flashcards

An interactive flashcard website for studying Customs Clearance I with 295 cards organized by topic.

## Features

- **Card Flipping Animation**: Click cards or press Space to flip and reveal answers
- **Topic Filtering**: Browse cards by 24 different topics
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Use arrow keys (← →) to navigate, Space to flip, Esc to close menus
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar shows your position in the deck
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Smooth Animations**: Motion.js powered transitions for a polished experience

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. Click on a card or press Space to flip it
3. Use arrow keys or buttons to navigate between cards
4. Click "Topics" to filter cards by subject area
5. Track your progress with the progress bar at the top
