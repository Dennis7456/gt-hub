# Customs in International Context - International Trade Tests

## Overview
Comprehensive test series covering international trade, customs procedures, trade documentation, payment methods, transport modes, Incoterms, and EPA agreements.

## Test Series Statistics
- **Total Tests**: 12
- **Questions per Test**: 50
- **Total Questions**: 600
- **Time Limits**: 45-60 minutes (progressive)
- **Difficulty**: Progressive across the series

## Test Structure

### Tests 1-3: Foundation Level (45 minutes each)
Focus on core concepts and terminology
- International trade definitions
- Import, export, and trade balance
- Types of trade and classification
- Players in international trade
- Basic trade processes

### Tests 4-8: Intermediate Level (50 minutes each)
Deeper understanding and application
- Trade documentation (commercial, transport, financial)
- Payment methods and risk allocation
- Transport modes and logistics
- Trade process steps
- Document categories and functions

### Tests 9-10: Advanced Level (55 minutes each)
Complex concepts and integration
- Incoterms 2020 (all 11 rules)
- EPA agreements (Kenya-EU, Kenya-UK)
- Risk and responsibility allocation
- Practical application scenarios

### Tests 11-12: Mastery Level (60 minutes each)
Comprehensive synthesis and expert application
- Mixed-topic integration
- Complex scenario analysis
- EPA challenges and mitigation
- Full trade transaction understanding

## Usage Instructions

### Starting a Test
1. Open `test-X/index.html` in your browser (where X is 1-12)
2. Review the test information
3. Click "Start Test" to begin
4. Timer starts automatically

### During the Test
- Select one answer per question
- Navigate between questions using the question grid
- Answered questions are highlighted
- Time remaining is displayed at the top

### Submitting
- Click "Submit Test" when ready
- Confirm submission (shows time remaining and unanswered questions)
- Cannot submit until confirmation

### After Submission
- View your score and grade
- Review all questions with correct answers
- Read explanations for each question
- Retake the test if desired
